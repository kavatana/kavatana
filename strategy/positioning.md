- **Primary CTA**: "See how I build" → links to `/projects`
- **Secondary CTA**: "Open to the right opportunity →" → links to `/contact`

## Tone Rules
- Confident, grounded.
- No superlatives.
- No "passionate about", no "love to code", no "wear many hats".
